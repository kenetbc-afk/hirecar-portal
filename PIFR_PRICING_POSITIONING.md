## Pricing Philosophy: Three Revenue Layers

### Layer 1: ACCESS (Monthly Membership)
**What it is:** The relationship. The right to be inside the system.
**What it is NOT:** The revenue center.

This is the equivalent of AAA membership or an insurance premium. Clients pay to be connected to the engine. When nothing is happening, they're monitored, protected, and positioned. When something happens — that's when the engine activates and real revenue flows.

**The membership exists so HIRECAR is already there when the incident hits.**

### Layer 2: ACTIVATION (Engagement Fees + Service Charges)
**What it is:** The engine fires up. An incident occurred — collections, deposit theft, identity fraud, credit denial, repo, insurance claim. The client needs PIFR to execute.

This is where the money is made. The engagement fee reflects the complexity of what the engine needs to do. It's not a "setup fee" — it's the cost of deploying a proprietary system against a specific problem.

**Clients pay because the problem is costing them more than the engagement fee every day it stays unresolved.**

### Layer 3: ACCELERATION (Tradelines + Ecosystem)
**What it is:** The file is clean. Now it needs to be built. Tradelines replace removed negatives with aged positive history. Funding becomes accessible. Co-hosting generates income.

This is the compounding layer. Post-cleanup, every tradeline purchase and ecosystem entry generates high-margin revenue from a client who is already bought in and seeing results.

**Clients pay because the ROI is immediate and tangible — score jumps, approvals, income.**

## Internal Operating Rules

### Engagement Fee Determination
1. Client completes intake → PIFR Readiness Score generated by engine (not self-reported)
2. Score maps to case complexity tier → engagement fee range
3. Advisor confirms within range — no discretion below floor
4. F&F verification: named active member referral required, 5 per member cap
5. Fee split: 50% at intake, 50% at first dispatch (chargeback defense)

### Tradeline Prescription
1. Minimum 60 days active membership + at least 1 successful dispute round
2. Engine analyzes cleaned file → generates tradeline recommendation
3. Advisor presents recommendation — "the engine recommends" not "would you like to buy"
4. No tradelines sold outside the engine recommendation (prevents cherry-picking)
5. Post-posting: automatic funding eligibility assessment triggered

### Tier Transitions
1. Minimum 3-month commitment per tier (no monthly oscillation)
2. Upgrades: immediate, prorated
3. Downgrades: only at commitment end, only if no active dispute cycle in progress
4. Cancellation: 30-day notice, active disputes completed through current cycle

### IP Protection
1. No case law citations shared verbally — "our proprietary methodology addresses this"
2. All calls include NDA/recording disclaimer
3. Portal shows status (pending/sent/responded/resolved) NOT strategy (which bureau, which round, which layer)
4. Staff trained: never confirm letter content, legal citations, or dispute sequencing
5. Bureau responses stored internally — client sees outcome, not the response text
6. Service agreement includes 2-year non-compete on credit services + NDA on methodology
